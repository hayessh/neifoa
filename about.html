INTRODUCTION:

ROLE OF THE ASSOCIATION

The purposes of the NEIFOA are: To establish and maintain an organization that will promote through the actions, attitude and leadership of its members, the maintaining of a high standard of ethics, good sportsmanship, respect and encouragement of fair play with cooperation and better understanding among officials, athletic directors, coaches and players. All rules, regulations and policies adopted by the Illinois High School Athletic Association (IHSA) and the National Federation of High School Athletic Association (NFHS ).

To sponsor, coordinate and promote programs that will provide training and continuing education to officials in order to improve their judgment, game management, knowledge of the rules, conditioning and opportunities for advancement while providing competent, well-trained and unbiased contest officials for the youths that are participating in youth leagues, junior high, freshman, and high school athletic contests. To recruit and encourage and provide an equal opportunity for a diversity of its members that reflect the community that NEIFOA serves, including, but not limited to, minority and female members, with respect to contest assignments, advancement, ratings and selection for leadership positions, regardless of sex, national origin, race, age, disability or religion.
