Welcome to Northeastern Illinois Football Offcials Association

ROLE OF THE ASSOCIATION

The Northeastern Illinois Football Officials Association is dedicated to serving the sport of football in the state of Illinois through the training and development of quality officials who will positively impact the sports in which they are involved. We aim to fulfill this mission by maintaining an organization that upholds the highest principles in supporting our members at every stage of their officiating career..

Vision

To be a premier officiating Association, in cooperation with our partner leagues, teams and organizations, the Northeastern Illinois Football Officials Association is committed to the ongoing process of elevating the standards of officiating through recruitment, education, mentoring, evaluations and accountability.

Play of the Week:

SITUATION:

Second and four at the B-15. B41 intercepts a forward pass at the B-2 and his momentum carries him into his end zone. While still in the end zone B41 fumbles the ball which rolls forward into the field of play. In the scramble for the ball it goes back into Team B’s end zone and over the end line.

Answer Below:

NFHS and NCAA RULING: In both rulings, this will be a safety and two points for Team A. The force (NFHS) or impetus (NCAA) for the ball being in the end zone the second time is from the fumble, and therefore Team B is responsible. (NFHS 8-5-1; 8-5-2b; NCAA 8-5-1; 8-7-1)
