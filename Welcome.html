Welcome to Northeastern Illinois Football Offcials Association

ROLE OF THE ASSOCIATION

The Northeastern Illinois Football Officials Association serves our local schools through providing the best officiating in the State of Illinois. Through officiating we look to enhance student learning, sportsmanship, safety and lifelong values. With integrity and professionalism as our foundation, we consistently govern the rules created by the Illinois High School Athletic Association and the National Federation of State High School Associations...

Vision

To be a premier officiating Association

In cooperation with our partner leagues, teams and organizations, the Northeastern Illinois Football Officials Association is committed to the ongoing process of elevating the standards of officiating through recruitment, education, mentoring, evaluations and accountability.

Play of the Week:

SITUATION:

Second and four at the B-15. B41 intercepts a forward pass at the B-2 and his momentum carries him into his end zone. While still in the end zone B41 fumbles the ball which rolls forward into the field of play. In the scramble for the ball it goes back into Team B’s end zone and over the end line.

Answer Below:

NFHS & NCAA RULING: In both rulings, this will be a safety and two points for Team A. The force (NFHS) or impetus (NCAA) for the ball being in the end zone the second time is from the fumble, and therefore Team B is responsible. (NFHS 8-5-1; 8-5-2b; NCAA 8-5-1; 8-7-1)
